Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use the Donezo mobile application (the "App").

Information We Collect

Authentication Data

We offer multiple authentication methods:

  1. Email/Password Registration:
    • Email address
    • Encrypted password
    • Account creation date
  2. Sign in with Apple:
    • Apple provided user identifier
    • Email address (if authorized by user)
    • Name (if authorized by user)
    • Account creation date
  3. Google Sign-In:
    • Google provided user identifier
    • Email address
    • Basic profile information
    • Account creation date
